Illusions are ideas created to distort an image in an attempt to decieve the eye. This can take form in many different ways; Graphic Design, Computer Coding & Programming, as well as other forms of Animation, share this notion of illusions. They decieve the eye into believing that any design that is moving, three-dimensional, or appears to be hyper-realistic actually takes form in two-dimensionionality.

This design shows exactly that. The lines anf forms created on the page are realistically two-dimensonal. Yet they are animated using coding to create an illusion that it is moving.
